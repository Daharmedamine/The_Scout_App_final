Build a web app for **FTC team scouting** with accounts tied to a team number.

## Core goal

FTC teams can **log in from any device**, create scouting entries for other teams during matches, and **store everything in a database** so it’s accessible anywhere.

## Authentication

* Start with a **Login page**:

  * Inputs: **Team Number** + **Password**
  * Button: **Log In**
  * Link: **Sign Up**
* **Sign Up page**:

  * Inputs: Team Number, Team Name (optional), Password, Confirm Password
  * Create an account for the team
* Passwords must be **securely stored (hashed)**.
* After login, users land on a dashboard.

## Database & Sync

* Use a database to store:

  * Team accounts (team number, hashed password, metadata)
  * Scouting reports created by that team
* All scouting reports are linked to the **owner team number** (the team that created the report).
* Because data is in a database, the same team can log in from any device and see their data.

## Scouting workflow

After logging in, users can:

1. Click **“New Scout”**
2. Enter the **Scouted Team Number**
3. Fill a scouting form with these sections/fields:

### Autonomous Section

* Starting position (where do they perform better? Far or Close)
* Preload action
* Auto scoring (points, accuracy, cycles)
* Navigation accuracy

### Tele-Op Section

* Cycles (of artifacts) / minute
* Scoring accuracy (average score per match)
* Consistency (scoring / # shots)
* Mechanism performance (intake, shooter, arm, etc.)

### Endgame Section

* Endgame task completion
* Reliability
* Time to complete
* Penalties taken

### Robot Performance Metrics

* Speed
* Reliability
* Defense ability
* Driver skill

### Optional

* Weight

### Other

* Strong and weak suits
* Better at defence or shooting?

## Saving & viewing

* Button: **Save Report**
* After saving, show confirmation and return to a list view:

  * “My Scouting Reports”
  * Filters/search by scouted team number
  * Click any report to view full details
  * Option to edit or delete (owner team only)

## Sharing with other teams

* Feature: **Share Report**

  * Input: “Other Team Number”
  * When shared, that other team can see the report after logging in.
* Permissions:

  * Owner team can edit/delete
  * Shared teams can view (read-only)

## UI pages (minimum)

* Login
* Sign Up
* Dashboard
* New Scout Form
* Report List
* Report Detail View
* Share dialog/page

## Tech expectations

* Use a modern stack (any is fine): frontend + backend + database.
* Make it responsive for phones/tablets (FTC pits).
* Validate inputs (team numbers are numeric, required fields, etc.).
* Keep the app simple, fast, and easy to use during competitions.
