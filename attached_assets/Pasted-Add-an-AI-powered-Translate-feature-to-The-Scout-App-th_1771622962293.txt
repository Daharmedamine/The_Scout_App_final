* Do NOT change: team numbers, scores, stats, percentages, formulas
* Keep the same sections and headings order
* Keep bullet points and formatting
* Keep FTC/robotics terms accurate (don‚Äôt rewrite meanings)

## Backend Translation Endpoint

Create:

* `POST /api/translate`

Input:

* `contentType` (report | robot_profile | ai_result | comp_summary)
* `contentId`
* `targetLanguageCode`
* `rawText` (or load by contentId on server)

Output:

* `translatedText`

Important:

* Call OpenAI from backend only (never from the mobile app)
* Validate permissions: user must be allowed to view this content before translating
